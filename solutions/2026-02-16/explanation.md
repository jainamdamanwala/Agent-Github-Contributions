# Container With Most Water

**ID:** 11  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**URL:** https://leetcode.com/problems/container-with-most-water/  

## Explanation

This problem can be solved by using a two-pointer technique. We start from both ends of the array and move the pointer of the shorter line towards the other end. The reason for this is that the area of the container is determined by the height of the shorter line. By moving the pointer of the shorter line, we can maximize the area of the container.
