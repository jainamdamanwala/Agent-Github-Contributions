# Best Time to Buy and Sell Stock

**ID:** 121  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**URL:** https://leetcode.com/problems/best-time-to-buy-and-sell-stock/  

## Explanation

This problem can be solved using dynamic programming. We maintain two variables, buy and sell, representing the maximum profit we can get after buying and selling the stock respectively. We iterate over the prices array and update the buy and sell variables accordingly.
